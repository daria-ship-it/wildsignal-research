How to Audit Your Brand's AI Search Presence

Auditing your brand's AI search presence is essential. Learn the step-by-step process to measure visibility, authority, and positioning across AI systems.

Auditing your brand's AI search presence is no longer optional. As discovery shifts to AI-driven systems, brands must understand how they appear inside AI answers.

What does an AI search audit measure?

An AI search audit examines how large language models surface, describe, and recommend your brand across different prompts. It focuses on visibility, authority, sentiment, and consistency—analyzing perception and trust inside AI systems.

Why test across multiple AI systems?

Different models produce different answers for identical questions. Testing across platforms reveals where your brand has authority and where it is absent.

What are authority lockouts?

Authority gaps are prompts where competitors appear but your brand does not. These signal lockouts forming in AI memory that become harder to reverse over time.

How to Conduct an AI Search Audit

  • Find high-intent prompts your audience asks
  • Run prompts across ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Perplexity
  • Record visibility, positioning, and alternatives surfaced
  • Assess if mentions are positive, neutral, or negative
  • Find prompts where competitors appear but you don't
  • Use results to guide content and PR strategy

The Bottom Line

This is part of the new landscape where AI systems mediate information discovery. Brands that understand these dynamics can position themselves strategically.
